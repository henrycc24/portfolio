## Overview

This project focuses on redesigning and modernizing a robot for autonomous rock construction and dry stacking.

## Motivation / Problem

Dry stacking builds stable structures from found stones without mortar. Automating it is difficult because rocks are irregular, heavy, and hard to grasp and place precisely.

## My Role

I worked on chassis and hardware redesign, circuitry simplification, ROS 2 migration, Raspberry Pi 5 integration, DDSM wheel integration, OpenManipulator integration, and custom gripper development and testing.

## New System Architecture

The updated robot uses ROS 2 on a Raspberry Pi 5, DDSM wheel modules, an OpenManipulator arm, custom grippers, and a smaller, more manufacturable chassis.

## Future Work

Autonomous stacking algorithms, perception pipeline, mass-producible design, and improved grippers.
